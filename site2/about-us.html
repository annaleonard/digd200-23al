About Us


Community Green is a community outreach, non-profit organization. Our mission is to empower communities to thrive in a clean and sustainable environment. We are dedicated to fostering a sense of environmental stewardship by providing information and accessible resources for community cleanups. Together, we envision cleaner communities where every member is equipped with the knowledge and tools needed to make a lasting impact on the well-being of our shared environment. At Community Green, we believe that by uniting in purpose, we can create a world where every community takes pride in nurturing and preserving the natural beauty that sustains us all.
