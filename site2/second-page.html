Keep Philadelphia Beautiful

Keep Philadelphia Beautiful can help you with workshops and community beautification efforts, including extra supplies and working with various City departments. Call 215-854-4000 or email info@keepphiladelphiabeautiful.org. Check out the resources section of our website at www.keepphiladelphiabeautiful.org/resources.

Fairmount Park Conservancy

Help LOVE Your Park with the Fairmount Park Conservancy! Visit www.myphillypark.org for additional information about how to get involved with your local park and Parks Friends Group.

Pennsylvania Horticultural Society

Want to start your own garden on your block, or learn how to plant and care for trees? Who better to help than the Pennsylvania Horticultural Society! Find them online at www.phsonline.org.

Young Involved Philadelphia

Young Involved Philadelphia works to foster civic engagement among millennials in Philadelphia. Visit their Civic Engagement Guide at www.yiphilly.org/resources for a list of neighborhood associations and other resources throughout the City.
